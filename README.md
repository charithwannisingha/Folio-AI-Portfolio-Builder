# 🚀 Folio - AI-Powered Developer Portfolio Builder

**Folio** is a next-generation, AI-driven portfolio infrastructure built for developers. Simply upload your CV, and our Groq AI engine instantly maps your skills and experience to deploy a stunning, highly optimized digital portfolio in under 30 seconds.

🌐 **Visit Website:** [https://folio.whitecoder.online/](https://folio.whitecoder.online/)

---

## ✨ Key Features

* 🧠 **Groq AI (LLaMA 3.3) Parsing:** Intelligent PDF extraction bypasses manual data entry, flawlessly mapping your timeline, skills, and projects into a structured schema.
* 🎨 **Premium Templates:** Apply award-winning, performance-optimized visual architectures ranging from minimalist grids to GSAP-powered WebGL environments.
* 🔗 **Custom Domain & DNS Routing:** Map any personal domain directly to your portfolio with automatic SSL provisioning.
* 💻 **Raw Code Injection:** Write and deploy raw HTML/CSS/JS directly to your portfolio endpoint for full control.
* ⚡ **Global CDN Integration:** Automated media optimization and zero-latency edge-network delivery via Cloudinary.
* 📊 **Telemetry Dashboard:** Built-in visitor tracking, geo-distribution, and device metrics.
* 🐙 **GitHub Sync:** Connect your GitHub profile to auto-populate repositories, languages, and contribution graphs in real-time.

---

## 🛠️ How It Works

1. **Initialize Data:** Drop your PDF CV into the engine. The AI parses the data instantly.
2. **Select Architecture:** Choose from a variety of visually stunning templates.
3. **Customize & Code:** Use the raw code editor to fine-tune your portfolio or let the AI do the heavy lifting.
4. **Deploy:** Connect your custom domain and go live globally in seconds.

---

## 💻 Technologies Used

* **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Tailwind CSS, GSAP, Three.js, WebGL
* **Backend & AI:** PHP, Node.js, Groq AI (LLaMA 3.3)
* **Database:** MySQL
* **Infrastructure:** Cloudinary (CDN), Docker, REST APIs
